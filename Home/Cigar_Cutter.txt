What is the Selmer Cigar Cutter?
A saxophone produced by the Selmer Company between 1930 and 1933.	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Selmer_Cigar_Cutter|[ref 1]]]
The Cigar Cutter is officially known as the Selmer Super Sax (SSS). [[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E_HrdDmoWKQ|[ref 4]]]
