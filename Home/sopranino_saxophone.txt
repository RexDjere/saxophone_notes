Created Thursday 26 August 2021

How much does a sporanino saxophone weigh?
A sopranino saxophone weighs approximately 1 pound 2 ounces (0.6 kilograms).

A sopranino saxophone is about equivalent in weight to what?
A sopranino saxophone weighs about the same as a soccer ball or football.
