Describe the bass saxophone.
THe bass saxophone is one of the largest members of the +saxophone_family - larger than the more commonly encountered [[Home:baritone saxophone|baritone saxophone]].	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bass_saxophone|[ref 1]]]

Describe the key of the bass saxophone.
The modern bass saxophone is a +transposing_insrument pitched in +Bb , and octave below the [[Home:tenor saxophone|tenor saxophone]].	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bass_saxophone|[ref 1]]]

When and where is the bass saxophone used?
The bass saxophone is not a commonly used instrument, but it is heard in some 1920s jazz recordings; in +free_jazz ; and occasionally in +concert_bands .	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bass_saxophone|[ref 1]]]
