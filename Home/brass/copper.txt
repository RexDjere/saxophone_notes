What is copper?
Copper is a chemical element with the symbok Cu (from Latin: cuprum) and atomic number 29.

What are the properties of copper?	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Copper|[ref 1]]]
* soft
* malleable
* [[+ductile]]
* high thermal conductivity
* high electrical conductivity


What is the historical significance of copper?
It is one of the first metals that humans worked.	[[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3nneL6zDEMA|[ref 2]]]
